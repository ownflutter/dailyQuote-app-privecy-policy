Privacy Policy

Effective: November 20, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how A Daily Quote handles your information. We keep personal data collection at zero while offering offline quotes, optional sharing, rating prompts, and limited banner & app‑open ads to support development.

Information We Collect

A Daily Quote does NOT collect, store, or share any personal information.
  • No account creation
  • No personal data (name, email, location, etc.)
  • No analytics or tracking
  • Core quote functionality works 100% offline

Data Storage

All quotes and app features run locally on your device. The only network activity is for ads and optional rating links.

Advertisements

To keep the app free, we display:

  • Banner Ads – shown at the bottom of select screens
  • App‑Open Ads – displayed briefly when you launch the app

Ads are served via privacy‑respecting networks (e.g., Google AdMob). We do not use personalized advertising, behavioral tracking, or any device identifiers for targeting. Ad behavior follows your device’s privacy and ad‑settings.

Sharing Quotes

You may share app using your device’s native share sheet. This feature:

  • Operates entirely on‑device
  • Collects no data about shares or recipients

Rating the App

Occasional prompts invite you to rate us on Google Play / App Store. Clicking opens the store’s review page; no rating data is sent to us.

Permissions

Required only for ads:

  • Internet – to load banner & app‑open ads
  • No other permissions (camera, location, contacts, storage)

Third‑Party Services

Limited to ad networks only (e.g., Google AdMob). No analytics, social SDKs, or cloud services.

Children’s Privacy

Safe for all ages. No personal data collection → full COPPA compliance. Parents can manage ad settings on the device.

Changes to This Policy

Updates appear in‑app and on store listings. Continued use means acceptance.

Data Security

No personal data = no privacy risk. Ad requests use standard HTTPS encryption.

Your Rights

Because we collect nothing:

  • No data to access, delete, export, or correct
  • Control ads via device settings

Consent

By using A Daily Quote Application, you agree to this policy.
